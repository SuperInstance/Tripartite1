## How It Works (Simple Version)

### Three Perspectives, One Answer

SuperInstance doesn't just give you the first answer it thinks of. It consults three specialized perspectives:

| Perspective | What It Does | Why It Matters |
|-------------|--------------|----------------|
| **Pathos** | Understands what you actually meant | No more "that's not what I asked" |
| **Logos** | Builds the technical solution | Deep expertise in your project |
| **Ethos** | Checks if it's true and feasible | No hallucinations, no broken code |

They must **agree** before you see an answer. This is why SuperInstance is more reliable than single-model AI.

### Privacy by Design

When SuperInstance needs cloud help:

1. **Before sending**: "Fix the bug in John's authentication code" becomes "Fix the bug in [USER_01]'s [CODE_BLOCK_A]"
2. **Cloud processes**: Works with anonymized version
3. **After receiving**: Swaps placeholders back to real values

**The cloud never sees your secrets.**
